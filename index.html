HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop

Date: December 10-11, 2021

Location: HEC Paris, France

Organizers: Jean-Noël Barrot, Johan Hombert

 

The HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop is a small-scale workshop that brings together researchers working in the field of entrepreneurship and innovation. It offers great opportunities for research discussions and interactions inside and outside the seminar room.

The workshop will take place on the HEC Paris campus on December 10-11, 2021. We welcome submissions on entrepreneurship, innovation and fintech.

Because the interactions inside and outside the seminar room are a key part of this event, the workshop will take place 100% face-to-face on the HEC campus (unless the sanitary situation deteriorates and prevents participants to travel).

The workshop will start on Friday, December 10 in the morning and end on Saturday, December 11 in the afternoon. Dinner will be held at a starred Michelin restaurant. Travel (economy class) and two hotel nights will be covered for presenters and discussants.

Submission deadline is October 10, 2021. Please submit your paper to hec-entrepreneurship@hec.fr. Decisions will be communicated by the end of October 2021.
