HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop

Date: December 9-10, 2022

Location: HEC Paris, France

Organizers: Johan Hombert, Simon Mayer

 

The HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop is a small-scale workshop that brings together researchers working in the field of entrepreneurship and innovation. It offers great opportunities for research discussions and interactions inside and outside the seminar room.

The workshop will take place on the HEC Paris campus on December 9-10, 2022. We welcome submissions on entrepreneurship, innovation, private equity, fintech, and related fields.

Because informal research interactions are a key part of this event, the workshop will take place 100% in-person on the HEC campus. Funding of travel expenses is available for PhD student speakers.

The workshop will start on Friday, December 9 in the morning and end on Saturday, December 10 in the afternoon. Dinner will be held at a starred Michelin restaurant.

Submission deadline is October 9, 2022. Please submit your paper to hec-entrepreneurship@hec.fr and name the PDF as LASTNAME_Firstname_Title.pdf. Decisions will be communicated by the end of October 2022.
