HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop

Date: December 14-15, 2023

Location: HEC Paris, France

Organizers: Johan Hombert, Jessica Jeffers

 

The HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop is a small-scale workshop that brings together researchers working in the field of entrepreneurship and innovation. It offers great opportunities for research discussions and interactions inside and outside the seminar room.

 

2023 Workshop Call For Paper

The workshop will take place on the HEC Paris campus on December 14-15, 2023. We welcome submissions on entrepreneurship, innovation, and their financing.

The workshop will start on Tuesday, December 14 with lunch and end on Friday, December 15 in the afternoon. Dinner will be held at a starred Michelin restaurant on Friday evening.

For those interested in AI and the intersection of entrepreneurship and AI, the workshop is scheduled back-to-back with the HEC Paris Workshop on AI and Entrepreneurship [insert link], organized by Tom Astebro and Carlos Serrano, which be held on Saturday, December 16.

Submission deadline is October 8, 2023. Please submit your paper to hec-entrepreneurship@hec.fr and name the PDF as LASTNAME_Firstname_Title.pdf. Decisions will be communicated by the end of October.
